# Business Definitions – NYC School Climate Data

**Student Response Rate**
The proportion of students who completed the NYC School Climate Survey.
This metric reflects student engagement and participation in school climate feedback efforts.

**Teacher Response Rate**
Percentage of teachers who responded to the School Climate Survey.
Represents staff engagement and culture signals within the school.

**Parent Response Rate**
Percentage of parents who responded to the survey.
Often used as a proxy for school–family engagement.

**District Climate Score**
Aggregated student/teacher/parent response rates at the district level.
Provides district leadership with topline engagement indicators.

**Borough Climate Score**
Climate engagement aggregated by borough.
Useful for comparing community-level trends.

**Vulnerability Score (SVI)**
Represents community-level vulnerability based on CDC-defined socioeconomic, demographic, and housing variables.
Used to understand how climate engagement aligns with community needs.

**High-SVI Tract**
A census tract classified as “High” vulnerability, signaling increased socioeconomic or demographic risk factors.

**School Climate Snapshot**
A school-level rolled-up view capturing engagement metrics for each school.
Used to identify high-performing schools or those requiring targeted interventions.

**Equity Insight**
Any analytic comparison that connects climate metrics with demographic or vulnerability measures at district or borough levels.
